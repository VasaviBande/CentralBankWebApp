About

Central Bank is an UK multinational, public sector banking and financial services company.

It is a government-owned corporation with its headquarters in London, UK.

This is the first ever large scale consolidation in the UK Banking Industry. With the merger, Central Bank will enter the league of top 50 global banks with a balance sheet size of ₹33 trillion, 278,000 employees, 420 million customers, and more than 24,000 branches and 59,000 ATMs.

CB's market share will increase to 22 percent from 17 per cent. It has 198 offices in 37 countries; 301 correspondents in 72 countries. The company is ranked 232nd on the Fortune Global 500 list of the world's biggest corporations as of 2016.
